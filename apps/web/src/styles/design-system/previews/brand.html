Brand

Who we are out loud.

We're the side of the creator economy that pays receipts, not promises. Our voice is direct, a little cheeky, and numbers-first. We don't promise influence — we pay for it.

Logo

Primary — on white. Wordmark only (no icon lockup yet).
Reversed — on pink-600. Use for covers, ads, event signage.
Gradient — hero moments only. Never on small sizes.
Dark — on slate-900. For embedded placements.

Minimum height: 20px digital / 6mm print. Clear-space = the height of the "S" on all sides. Don't stretch, recolor outside of these four treatments, or add drop shadows.

Voice — do / don't

✓ We say it like this

"You posted. It landed. Here's your $62."

Concrete · past tense · names the number.

✕ We don't say it like this

"Unlock the power of authentic creator partnerships."

Vague · buzzword-y · promises nothing.

✓ We say it like this

"2 cents per click. Ends Friday. 347 creators in."

Hard numbers · a deadline · social proof in that order.

✕ We don't say it like this

"Join thousands of creators monetizing their influence today!"

Exclamation, "monetize," vague count.

✓ We say it like this

"Your link didn't verify. Here's why and what to fix."

Owns the failure · points to the fix · no apologies.

✕ We don't say it like this

"Oops! Something went wrong 😅 We'll look into it soon."

Cutesy · vague · dodges.

Three voice pillars

01
Receipts-first

Always lead with the number — dollars, clicks, hours. If we can't show a number, we probably shouldn't be saying it.

02
Friendly, not fake

Contractions, second person, sentence-case. No emoji in product. No "woohoo." We're in on the joke, not the butt of it.

03
Short > clever

If a button label is longer than three words, cut one. If a heading is longer than ten, rewrite it.

Imagery & illustration

Photography
[ creator portrait · phone-in-hand ]

Real creators, phones visible, natural light. Never stocky. Tight crops on hands, faces, and screens. Over-saturate reds and pinks by ~10%.

Illustration

Geometric, flat, 2-color (pink + blue + one neutral). No gradients in illustration — save gradients for type and progress bars.
