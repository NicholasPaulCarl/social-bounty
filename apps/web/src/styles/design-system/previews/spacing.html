Spacing · Radii · Shadows

A 4-point grid, three radii, four elevations.

Every gap is a multiple of 4px. Every rounded corner is one of three sizes. Cards float just enough to feel tactile without shouting.

Spacing — 4px grid

Base unit 4px. Tokens --space-1…--space-24. Within a card use 1–4. Between cards use 4–6. Between sections use 8–16. Never eyeball — always snap to a token.

Radii

Pills are a brand signal — use --radius-full on CTAs, badges, chips. Default card is --radius-xl (16px). Feature cards go --radius-3xl (24px). Never mix two different non-full radii within one component.

Elevation — 4 levels + 2 glows

Neutral shadows are black with low alpha. Never invent a custom shadow. The two colored glows (pink, blue) are ONLY for primary CTAs and the hero card — used sparingly.
