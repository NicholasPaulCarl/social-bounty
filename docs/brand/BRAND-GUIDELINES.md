# Social Bounty — Brand Guidelines

> Quick-reference brand guide for developers, designers, and AI agents building Social Bounty.

---

## 1. What We Are

Social Bounty is a bounty-based marketplace where **Brands** post small, rewarding tasks and **Hunters** complete them daily for real income. We cut out agencies, kill gatekeeping, and make UGC simple, direct, and fun.

**Positioning**: "The bounty board for the internet."

**Tagline**: "Hunt. Create. Earn."

---

## 2. Brand Archetype: The Jester

We're playful, clever, and energising — not corporate, not chaotic.

**We are**: Witty, optimistic, inclusive, direct, reliable when it counts.
**We are NOT**: Silly, mocking, dismissive, chaotic, or exclusive.

**The rule**: If the humour were removed, the message must still make complete sense.

---

## 3. Terminology

| Use This | Never This |
|---|---|
| **Hunter** | User, participant, influencer, creator |
| **Bounty** | Task, job, gig, campaign |
| **Bounty Board** | Marketplace, listings, feed |
| **Submission** | Entry, deliverable, upload |
| **Proof** | Evidence, attachment |
| **Reward** | Payment, payout (except in brand-admin contexts) |
| **Brand** | Client, company, advertiser |
| **Claim** | Apply, sign up, accept |

**Internal mapping**: Database `PARTICIPANT` = **Hunter** in UI. `Brand` = **Brand** in casual copy.

## 4. Colour Palette

### Primary — Pink
- `primary-500`: `#ec4899` — buttons, links, key accents
- `primary-600`: `#db2777` — hover/active
- `primary-700`: `#be185d` — pressed, focus rings

### Secondary — Blue
- `secondary-500`: `#3b82f6`
- `secondary-600`: `#2563eb`

### Status
- Success: `#22c55e` — Approved, Live, earned
- Warning: `#f59e0b` — Pending, Needs More Info
- Danger: `#ef4444` — Rejected, Error, Suspended
- Info: `#3b82f6` — Submitted, informational

### Reward Accent
- Gold: `#f59e0b` — reward amounts, achievement badges

## 6. Words We Love vs Avoid

**Love**: hunt, earn, claim, crush it, drop, fresh, fast, real, proof, reward, board, quest, level up, unlock

**Avoid**: leverage, synergy, utilise, stakeholder, deliverable, optimise, circle back, pipeline, holistic, solution, disrupt, ecosystem
