## Notes on selection

- **Filter applied**: excluded nodes with `id` prefixed `community:` and nodes whose label starts with `Community `.
- **Label collisions**: when two nodes shared a label (e.g. `.create()`, `.update()`, `page.tsx`, `index.ts`) the highest-degree one was kept.
- **File slug** = node id with `/` and `:` replaced by `_`. Stable across re-clusterings.
